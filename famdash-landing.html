Features

Everything your family needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Six powerful modules, one calm interface. FamDash replaces the group chat, the shared notes app, the birthday calendar, and the "where are you?" text.

🏠

Family Location

See every family member with live addresses and real-time distances. Gold = distance from home. Blue = distance from you. Green = they're home safe.

See All Features ↓
Live GPS location updates for every family member
Two distance badges — from home and from you
Instant "At Home" status when someone arrives
One-tap driving directions to any member
Choose Apple Maps or Google Maps per member
Customize each member's card color and style
Auto-sorted by distance — closest member first
🛒

Shopping Lists

Create a list — your family sees it instantly. Add items while someone's at the store. A green dot tells them something new arrived. Confetti flies when the last item is checked.

See All Features ↓
Real-time sync — family sees changes instantly
Green dot alerts when items are added mid-shop
Drag to reorder items by aisle or priority
Mark items Not Available — restore with one swipe
Tap to rename any item on the fly
Color-coded status icons — new, in progress, complete
Duplicate any list with one swipe — fresh start
Smart duplicate check warns if list exists today
Confetti celebration when list is completed
Completed lists auto-archive after set days
Push notifications for started, added, and completed
🎂

Celebrations

Birthdays, anniversaries, and everything worth celebrating. Set it once — FamDash remembers forever. When today's celebration passes, it auto-cycles to next year. No re-adding. Ever.

See All Features ↓
Track birthdays, anniversaries, and custom events
Color-coded urgency — see what's coming at a glance
Day of week displayed — plan a party or send a text
Auto-cycles to next year — never re-add anything
Monthly digest notification on the 1st of each month
7-day heads-up so you can plan ahead
Day-of reminder at 8 AM — never forget again
Swipe to edit name, type, or date anytime
Swipe to delete celebrations you no longer need

Smart Reminders

Six color-coded categories. Smart dates that speak human — "In 15m," "Tomorrow at 9 AM." Overdue reminders sit in red at the top. Impossible to ignore.

See All Features ↓
Six color-coded categories for instant recognition
Overdue reminders pinned to the top in red
Smart sections — Today, This Week, and Later
Minute-level precision — "In 15m", "23m ago"
Set daily, weekly, or monthly recurring reminders
Next occurrence previews automatically
Family reminders notify every member
Personal reminders stay completely private
Dashboard with live overdue/today/upcoming counts
Swipe to edit title, category, date, or recurrence
Auto-cleanup of completed reminders after set days

To-Do's

Family to-do's everyone can see and complete. Personal to-do's visible only to you. Completed items collapse and auto-clean after your chosen number of days.

See All Features ↓
Family to-do's visible and completable by everyone
Personal to-do's stay completely private to you
See who completed each task and when
Uncheck completed items to restore them
Swipe to edit title or switch personal/family
Completed section collapses for a clean view
Auto-cleanup removes old completed items
Push notifications for new family to-do's
